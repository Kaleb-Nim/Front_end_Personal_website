My Secondary School life

(Evergreen Secondary School)

Throughout the 4 years, I've always found myself in new and more challenging circumstances.

I was introduced to the school environment/facilities, teachers, and CCAs. The orientation camp was also a means for us new classmates to bond over games which ended off with a camfire event.
