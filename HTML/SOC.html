SP SOC

(Applied AI and Analytics)

Although I'd only spent 7weeks in SP SOC, I've fallen in love with the lifestyle, environment and people. I consider my lectures to be experienced, nurturing and hands-on which helped pushed me to discover the wondrous world of IT.

I've learnt a considerable chunk from the common SOC modules which further increases my passion for IT. Overall, I have thoroughly enjoy my time in SP SOC thus far and have no regrets about choosing this field to spend the next 3 years of my life.

Bits and Pieces of My Polytechnic Lifestyle

Hilltop Haven

Hilltop Haven is currently my Favourite Place in SP.
It has a conducive environment being fully air-conditioned and even has a small shop that sells snacks and drinks.
I will usually stay there every day to study till about 5.30 pm.

Friend Group

I was fortunate enough to be able to make new friends as soon I entered SP. Consequently, we then organized "Makan"/exploration sessions every Tuesday morning ( before our 12pm class ) where we would travel to all the different Food-courts SP has to offer to check out the environment and the food.

Studying Styles

I definitely prefer the learning style of SP compared to Secondary School.
For most modules, lessons are conducted using the "Flipped Learning" style.
This implies that students are supposed to go through the lesson material themselves beforehand

Timetable

I feel grateful that the timetable I was assigned for Y1 Sem1 is well structured.
Firstly, there is no class on Thursdays which allows me to have a break and additional time to catch up/progress ahead in my studies.
Secondly, the late start on Tuesdays enables me to sleep in that day.
Lastly, there are no huge gaps in between lessons.
